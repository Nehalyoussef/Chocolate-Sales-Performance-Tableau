## Project Overview
This project presents an **interactive sales performance dashboard for global chocolate sales**, designed to track revenue across countries, evaluate **top-performing sales representatives**, and analyze **shipment distribution** worldwide.

The dashboard helps stakeholders understand regional sales performance, identify high-impact salespeople, and gain insights into logistics and product demand using clear, data-driven visualizations.

## Tools Used
- Tableau  
  - Interactive dashboard design  
  - Calculated fields and aggregations  
  - Filters and visual-level interactivity  
  - Geographic (map-based) visualizations  
- Sales analytics and performance analysis concepts

## Dataset Description
- **Type:** Chocolate sales transaction data  
- **Scope:** Global, multi-country sales records  
- **Key attributes include:**
  - Country  
  - Sales representative  
  - Chocolate product type (e.g., dark & pure variants)  
  - Sales amount  
  - Boxes shipped  
  - Transaction count  

The dataset represents **global chocolate sales activity**, commonly used in analytics practice to demonstrate sales performance tracking, regional comparison, and logistics analysis.

## Dashboard Components & Analysis

### Sales by Country
- Compares total sales amounts across countries
- Highlights top-performing markets such as **Australia, the UK, India, and the USA**
- Supports regional performance evaluation and market prioritization

### Top 10 Sales Representatives
- Ranks the highest-performing salespeople by total sales amount
- Identifies key contributors to overall revenue
- Useful for incentive planning, performance evaluation, and sales strategy optimization

### Business Summary KPIs
- Total sales amount  
- Total boxes shipped  
- Number of sales records  

Provides a **high-level snapshot** of overall business performance.

### Salesperson Performance by Product
- Displays average sales amount per salesperson by product type
- Helps assess:
  - Product popularity  
  - Salesperson-product alignment  
  - Individual sales effectiveness  

### Boxes Shipped by Country (Geographic View)
- World map visualizing shipment volume across regions
- Highlights logistics distribution and regional demand intensity
- Supports supply chain and fulfillment planning decisions

## Key Insights
- Sales performance varies significantly across regions, with a small number of countries driving the majority of revenue
- A limited group of sales representatives contributes disproportionately to total sales
- Certain chocolate product variants consistently generate higher average sales
- Shipment volume aligns closely with top-performing markets, reflecting regional demand patterns
